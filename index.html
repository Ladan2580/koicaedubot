Privacy Policy

AI for At-Risk Student Identification

Last Updated:

Introduction

This application "KoicaEduBot" was developed as part of a Master's degree research project in Applied Artificial Intelligence at Hanyang University, South Korea. The App provides real-time predictions of student at-risk status using machine learning models without storing any user data.

Important Privacy Assurance: This application does not store any user information permanently. All data you provide is used exclusively for real-time prediction via our trained machine learning model to identify student at-risk status. Once the prediction is generated, your input data is immediately discarded and not retained on our servers or in any persistent storage.

Information Processing

Data Flow

The complete data processing workflow:

  1. You temporarily input educational information
  2. The data is securely transmitted to our prediction service
  3. Our machine learning model generates an at-risk assessment
  4. The results are displayed to you
  5. All input data is immediately and permanently deleted

Data Used for Prediction

The following data points are used temporarily for generating predictions:

Data Category Purpose in Prediction Model
Age Demographic factor in risk assessment
Gender Demographic factor in risk assessment
School Location Geographical educational context
Attendance Rate Primary indicator of engagement
Previous Term Score Academic performance baseline
Study Hours Per Week Learning behavior indicator
Parental Education Level Socio-educational factor
Household Income Level Socio-economic factor
Access To Internet Digital resource availability

Data Protection Guarantee

No Data Retention Policy: We guarantee that none of your information is stored after your prediction is complete. This includes:

  • No personal information stored on our servers
  • No prediction inputs logged or saved
  • No results associated with individual users
  • No persistent cookies or tracking mechanisms

Technical Safeguards

During the brief processing period, your data is protected by:

  • End-to-end encryption during transmission
  • Memory-only processing (no disk writes)
  • Automatic data purging after response
  • Regular security audits of the prediction service

Research Purpose

This application was developed exclusively for academic research as part of a Master's thesis in Applied Artificial Intelligence at Hanyang University. The research focuses on:

  • Transparent AI predictions using SHAP values
  • LLM (Gemini) explanations for model outputs
  • Chatbot interfaces for educational analytics

Non-commercial Use: This is a purely academic project with no commercial aspects. The application does not and will never contain advertisements or monetization features.

Third-Party Services

The App uses the following services that may process data temporarily:

  • Google Gemini API: For generating model explanations (data is not stored by Google)
  • Expo Application Services: For app distribution (no access to prediction data)

All third-party services are used in compliance with their data processing policies and Korean privacy regulations.
